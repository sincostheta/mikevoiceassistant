About MIKE

MIKE is an open-source AI-powered voice assistant and smart speaker platform, meticulously engineered around the versatile ESP-32 microcontroller. It integrates advanced Perplexity AI technology to deliver sophisticated natural language understanding and contextual intelligence. Additionally, MIKE offers seamless control over Spotify music playback, enabling users to effortlessly manage their audio experience through intuitive voice commands. Designed for flexibility and extensibility, MIKE serves as a cutting-edge solution for personalized digital assistance. Github Repository: https://github.com/sincostheta/mikevoiceassistant

AI Voice Recognition

Processes natural language with deep learning for accurate responses.

How to Set Up MIKE

  • Turn MIKE on and wait for 30 seconds.
  • Go to the WIFI Menu of your device and connect to "MIKE-Setup" with the password "mike123".
  • It should open a website in your browser, if it doesn't open automatically, go to 192.168.4.1 from your preferred web browser.
  • Enter your wifi name and the wifi password to the given blanks in the website and submit the form.
  • Wait for about a minute to initialize and start using!

How to Use MIKE Properly

  • Use the wake word "MIKE" for it to respond to you (customizable).
  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace for better recognition.
  • Wait for the blue LED to light up before issuing a command.
  • Keep MIKE within 1 metre radius for optimal voice pickup.
  • Keep MIKE in quiet environments with minimal background noise.
